Never Grow Up

Welcome to my passion project! I am Riley Radle, a junior computer science major and art minor at the University of Wisconsin La Crosse. I grew up in a rural town named Durand in western Wisconsin. I never really knew what I wanted to be when I grew up, but I always knew that whatever my passion was, I would chase it no matter the cost! This site is an extension of that ideal, and I am so glad you were able to be apart of my dream, whether through a purchase, or just visiting this site!

A dream and a purpose

I was introduced to art by a chance encounter with one of my calculus peers. He said I should give it a shot, and little did I know that it would quickly become a life long passion. I decided that --although a strange combination-- I could combine my love of art with my knowledge of computers. This gave me the ability to promote my work and several extremely exciting career paths. I currently aspire to create digital art, and computer graphics for movies and games.
